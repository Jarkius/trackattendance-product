# ## Awakening Lineage Discovery — Ask Before You Trace

## Awakening Lineage Discovery — Ask Before You Trace

When awakening a new Oracle, ask the human about their ecosystem's history before drafting any identity or announcement. They know their own lineage better than any automated trace can discover.

During Oracle-The-Matrix's awakening, the standard ancestor tracing (nat-brain-oracle, oracle-v2) missed the actual lineage: oracle-philosophy-book as the true origin, The-Oracle-Construct as the living parent, and matrix-seed as the portable extract. The human corrected the AI twice.

**The Pattern**: Before writing identity or announcements, ask "What repos led to this Oracle?" and "Where did the philosophy come from?" Then trace what they point you to. Write with the human's narrative as the backbone, not the AI's best guess.

This applies beyond awakening: whenever synthesizing a narrative about a user's work, their understanding of their own path is primary data.
