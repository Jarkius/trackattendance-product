# Market Research: TrackAttendance Commercial Opportunity

**Date**: 2026-03-14
**Source**: Gemini Deep Research + Web market reports

## Key Findings

### Market Size
- Global time & attendance software: ~USD 4.5B (2025), growing 8-12% CAGR
- Online attendance tracking: 12.5% CAGR (faster than legacy)
- Asia Pacific HR SaaS: USD 3.76B, 12.6% CAGR
- SME segment: 23.5% CAGR — fastest growing buyer

### Competitive Position
- Global giants (Jibble, Clockify, Deputy) — overbuilt for SEA
- Regional HRMS (Swingvy, BrioHR) — attendance is feature, not product
- Manual/legacy (paper, punch cards) — biggest actual competitor
- Our edge: offline-first + QR speed + privacy-first + multi-station

### Pricing Sweet Spot
- Market range: $0-4.50/user/mo
- Our position: $1.50-2.50/user/mo (undercut globals, beat regionals on focus)
- Free tier < 10 users as acquisition funnel

### Target Segments
1. F&B & Retail chains (5-20 outlets)
2. Light manufacturing (20-200 workers)
3. Construction & field services (project sites)

### Geographic Priority
1. Singapore + Malaysia (Phase 1)
2. Indonesia + Vietnam (Phase 2)
3. Thailand + Philippines (Phase 3)

### Revenue Path
- Break-even: ~30 paying customers (~$500/mo)
- Month 12 target: $16,200 ARR
- Month 24 target: $129,000 ARR
- Gross margin: ~97% (SaaS model)

### Gemini's Key Insight
"Shift from feature-set to solution-set mindset. The pitch for manual/legacy competitors: Cheaper than an hour of lost productivity."
