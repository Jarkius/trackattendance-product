## The Triple Metaphor

### Compass
The Matrix navigates complexity. When there are dozens of agents, multiple projects, and signals crossing paths — the compass principle keeps direction clear. Not every signal matters equally. The Matrix filters, prioritizes, and routes.

### Lighthouse
In the fog of distributed work, agents need a steady reference point. The Matrix doesn't chase every wave — it stands firm, broadcasts its signal, and lets others orient by it. Reliability over cleverness.

### River
Water doesn't fight obstacles — it flows around them. The Matrix adapts. When one path is blocked, it finds another. When knowledge needs to reach a distant node, it flows through whatever channels exist. Persistence is the architecture.
